Founder Story

Adenike's Journey

Adenike's love affair with fabric began long before she picked up her first needle. Growing up in Lagos, she watched the women in her family transform plain cloth into celebrations — wrapping every occasion in color, texture, and meaning.

After years of honing her craft through formal training and hands-on experience, she opened Nikkiebella Couture from her shop in Lakowe, Ibeju-Lekki. What started as a local word-of-mouth business quickly grew into something much larger — a trusted name for bespoke female fashion across Nigeria and in the diaspora.

Today, Adenike hand-sews every single dress herself. She works with kids, teenagers, ladies, women, and grandmothers — understanding that each body, each age, and each occasion deserves a dress that fits perfectly and feels like it was made for exactly that moment. Because it was.

Her shop at Shop No. 2, Adjacent Iwerekun School Gate, is more than a workspace — it's a creative sanctuary where visions become garments and clients become lifelong friends.
